Frequently Asked Questions

At Space Parking, we understand that you may have questions about our services. Here's a quick guide to address some common queries:

How do I make a reservation?

Making a reservation is simple! Visit our website or use our user-friendly app. Select your desired date, time, and location, then click 'Reserve.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We prioritize the security of your payment details. Our system uses industry-standard encryption to ensure your information is safe.

Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

Yes, you can! Log in to your account and find the reservation. From there, you can modify details or cancel as needed.

What happens if I arrive late for my reservation?

Don't worry! Your reserved spot will be held for a grace period. If you anticipate a significant delay, please contact our support team.

Are there any additional fees?

Our pricing is transparent. The total cost is displayed during the reservation process, so there are no surprises. No hidden fees, guaranteed.

Is Colombo Space Parking available 24/7?

Yes, our services are available around the clock. Whether it's early morning or late at night, we've got you covered.

What if I have trouble with my reservation?

Our customer support team is ready to assist you. Contact us via the app or website, and we'll promptly address any issues you encounter.

How secure is the parking facility?

Your security is our priority. Our facilities are equipped with surveillance and lighting to ensure a safe parking environment.
